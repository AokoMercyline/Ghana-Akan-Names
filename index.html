Akan Names

Ghana Akan Birthday

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first
name as a 'day name' which
corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
Here are Ghanian day names.

Men

  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
  • Sunday: Kwasi

Women

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
